Karel's four types of knowledge
📚
Training knowledge
Patterns baked in from training data. Available in every conversation, but frozen, cannot be updated by talking to him.
📅
Knowledge cutoff
Anything after the training cutoff date is unknown to Karel, unless you tell him in the conversation.
🧹
No session memory
Each conversation starts fresh. Karel has no recollection of previous chats. Memory must be built as a product feature.
📋
Context window
Real-time data can be given to Karel in the prompt, but it must be provided explicitly, every single time.
