Temperature as a product decision

Low temp (0.0 – 0.3)
Consistent, safe, predictable. Use for: fraud alerts, legal notices, compliance messages, account confirmations.
Medium temp (0.4 – 0.7)
Natural and varied while staying on-topic. Use for: general customer support, FAQ responses, product recommendations.
High temp (0.8 – 1.0+)
Creative and surprising, but unpredictable. Use for: brainstorming tools, creative writing assistants, ideation.
