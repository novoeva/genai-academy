Live Simulation The Temperature Dial Sub-chapter 2

Temperature controls how randomly the model samples its next word. Low temperature collapses the choice to one dominant word. High temperature spreads probability across many candidates, more creative, more unpredictable. Drag the slider and watch what happens.
